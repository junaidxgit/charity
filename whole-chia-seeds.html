Why you'll love Whole Chia Seeds

These versatile seeds expand up to 10× their weight in liquid, delivering satiating texture to overnight oats, smoothies, and vegan desserts. Lightly nutty in flavour, they complement both sweet and savoury recipes while supporting hydration.

Each batch is cold-cleaned to preserve fragile omega oils and stored in recyclable pouches with a moisture barrier so you experience optimal crunch with every scoop.

Stir 3 tablespoons into plant milk and let it rest for 20 minutes to create an instant chia pudding rich in fibre and heart-friendly fats.
